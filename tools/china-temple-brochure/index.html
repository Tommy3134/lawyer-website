Our Destinations

Four iconic Buddhist sites spanning sacred mountains and cosmopolitan Shanghai — each offering a distinct dimension of China's living spiritual heritage.

Mount Putuo

普陀山 · Zhejiang Province Sacred Island

A sacred island in the Zhoushan Archipelago and the earthly abode of Guanyin, the Bodhisattva of Compassion — the most beloved figure in East Asian Buddhism. For over 1,000 years, pilgrims have crossed the sea to this island sanctuary, home to 30 active temples and the iconic 33-meter gilded Nanhai Guanyin statue.

The founding legend is irresistible: in the 9th century, a Japanese monk's ship was halted by a miraculous storm as he tried to carry a Guanyin statue home. The statue "refused to leave" — and the island became sacred ground.

Key Sites

Puji Temple, Fayu Temple, Huiji Temple (summit), Nanhai Guanyin Statue, Chaoyin Cave, Thousand-Step Beach

Experiences

Temple ceremonies, incense offerings, vegetarian monastery meals, coastal meditation walks, sunrise viewing

Getting There

Ferry from Zhujiajian (Zhoushan) — 15 min fast boat. Zhoushan accessible from Shanghai (4h drive) or Ningbo (2h)

Best Seasons

April–June and September–November. Guanyin festival dates (lunar 2nd, 6th, 9th month 19th) for cultural immersion

Mount Emei

峨眉山 · Sichuan Province UNESCO Heritage

A UNESCO World Heritage Site rising to 3,079 meters, Mount Emei is the sacred abode of Samantabhadra, the Bodhisattva of Universal Virtue. Buddhism arrived here in the 1st century AD, making it one of China's earliest Buddhist sites. The Golden Summit, crowned by a 48-meter gold-plated Samantabhadra statue, offers one of the most celebrated sunrise experiences in all of China.

The mountain spans subtropical to subalpine zones with over 3,200 plant species — a living encyclopedia of biodiversity alongside 2,000 years of spiritual heritage.

Key Sites

Golden Summit (Jinding), Wannian Temple (Song Dynasty bronze elephant), Baoguo Temple, Qingyin Pavilion, Monkey Zone

Experiences

Summit sunrise, sea of clouds, temple overnight stays, vegetarian cuisine, hiking trails, meditation retreats

Getting There

High-speed train from Chengdu: 1–1.5 hours. Cable car to Golden Summit available. Chengdu has major international airport

Best Seasons

Spring (azaleas), Autumn (clear skies, best sunrise). Winter summit snow is spectacular. Summer is cool retreat season

Jing'an Temple

静安寺 · Shanghai Urban Sanctuary

Founded circa 247 AD, Jing'an Temple is nearly 1,800 years old — yet it sits directly on Nanjing West Road, Shanghai's premier boulevard, surrounded by luxury malls and glass towers. The contrast of golden Song Dynasty rooflines against a modern skyline is one of the most striking visual experiences in China.

The temple houses a 15-ton silver Sakyamuni Buddha and was meticulously rebuilt in 2010–2014 using traditional methods with Burmese teak. It remains an active Chan (Zen) Buddhist monastery.

Key Sites

Mahavira Hall (silver Buddha), Ashoka Pagoda, Hall of Heavenly Kings, historic Bubbling Spring, adjacent Jing'an Park

Experiences

Incense offerings, Buddhist ceremonies, Gongdelin vegetarian restaurant (est. 1922), combine with Nanjing Road shopping

Jade Buddha Temple

玉佛禅寺 · Shanghai Living Monastery

Home to two exquisite jade Buddha statues brought from Burma in 1882 — a 1.95-meter seated Buddha carved from a single piece of white jade, and a reclining Buddha depicting the moment of enlightenment. The temple is one of Shanghai's most active Buddhist monasteries with over 70 resident monks.

Renovated in 2017 with the seated jade Buddha carefully relocated to a new earthquake-proof hall at ground level, the temple blends preservation with accessibility.

Key Sites

Jade Buddha Hall, Reclining Buddha Chamber, Grand Hall, Sutra-Reciting Hall, calligraphy and Buddhist art collections

Experiences

Renowned vegetarian restaurant, monk-led temple tours, Buddhist art appreciation, calligraphy workshops, meditation sessions

Sacred Mountains Pilgrimage

10 Days · Shanghai → Putuo → Emei → Chengdu Signature Tour
Day 1

Airport meet-and-greet, transfer to hotel. Evening orientation walk along the Bund.

Day 2

Shanghai Temples

Day 3

Shanghai → Putuo Mountain

Morning transfer to Zhoushan, ferry to Putuo Island. Afternoon: Puji Temple, lotus pond, incense offering ceremony. Overnight on island.

Day 4

Putuo Mountain Full Day

Sunrise at Hundred-Step Beach. Morning: Fayu Temple, hike to Huiji Temple at summit. Afternoon: Nanhai Guanyin Statue, Chaoyin Cave. Vegetarian dinner at monastery. Optional evening chanting ceremony.

Day 5

Putuo → Shanghai → Chengdu

Morning ferry back. Transfer to airport, fly to Chengdu. Evening: Sichuan welcome dinner, Jinli Ancient Street walk.

Day 6

Chengdu → Mount Emei

High-speed train to Emei. Visit Baoguo Temple (gateway temple) and Mount Emei Museum. Afternoon: Qingyin Pavilion, Natural Monkey Zone. Overnight at mountain hotel.

Day 7

Mount Emei — Golden Summit

Early cable car to Golden Summit for sunrise and sea of clouds. Visit Huazang Temple and the 48m Samantabhadra statue. Afternoon: Wannian Temple, view the 1,000-year-old bronze elephant Buddha. Temple stay overnight (optional, standard hotel alternative available).

Day 8

Mount Emei — Meditation & Nature

Morning meditation session at temple. Guided nature walk through ancient forests. Afternoon: Leshan Giant Buddha (UNESCO, optional add-on). Return to Chengdu.

Day 9

Chengdu Free Day

Optional: Giant Panda Base, Wenshu Monastery, tea house experience, Sichuan cooking class. Farewell vegetarian banquet.

Day 10

Departure

Transfer to Chengdu airport. End of tour.
